Medical Aid Programmes

Does your medical aid cover a free dietitian consultation?

Many South Africans don't know they already qualify for free dietitian consultations through their medical aid. Check your scheme below.

Discovery
Diabetes Care Programme
If registered with the Chronic Illness Benefit for Diabetes, you can join the Diabetes Care programme — unlocking cover for additional dietitian consultations.
Maternity: Post-pregnancy
At 6 weeks post delivery you are eligible for a free dietitian consultation. After birth you also qualify for a free lactation consultation. *Discovery pays a fixed amount — a co-payment may apply.
Medihelp
Weight Management Programme
Members with a BMI over 30 qualify for an additional dietitian consultation covered by Medihelp on all benefit options. Register for HealthPrint and get a verified BMI from Clicks or Dis-Chem to activate.
Diabetes Programme
Members with Diabetes qualify for 1 consultation with a dietitian.
Fedhealth
Weight Management Programme
Access to 2 dietitian consults after a biokineticist visit. You qualify if you have a BMI >25, men waist >102cm or women >88cm, or BMI 25–29.
Bonitas
Health Assessment Benefit
Once you have completed a wellness screening or online wellness questionnaire (on the Bonitas Member App), you can access the Benefit Booster to pay for out-of-hospital claims — including a dietitian consultation.
Bestmed
Tempo Wellness Benefits
Complete your free health risk assessment (HRA) at any Dis-Chem, Clicks, PnP, Van Heerden, Sparkport or selected Alpha Pharm pharmacies to unlock 2 dietitian consultations for beneficiaries over 18, plus 1 family nutritional assessment — free.
Bankmed
Personal Health Assessment (PHA)
If categorised as medium- or high-risk by the PHA, or have a BMI >30, you have access to 2 dietitian consultations of 30 minutes each, plus 2 biokineticist consultations.
Polmed
Weight Management Programme
Access to 2 dietitian consults amongst other services. You qualify if you have a BMI >30, men waist >102cm or women >88cm, or BMI 25–29 with diabetes.

Not sure if your medical aid covers dietitian consultations? Contact us and we'll help you find out.

FAQ

Common Questions

How do virtual consultations work?
After you book, we send you a secure video call link. No software to install — it works in your browser. Sessions are the same length and quality as in-person visits, and you receive your plan digitally afterward.
How does payment work for consultations?
Consultations are not paid online. After your session, we send you a detailed invoice which you submit to your medical aid for reimbursement. Most major South African medical aids cover registered dietitian consultations.
How long is an initial dietitian session?
Initial sessions are typically 60 minutes — enough time for a thorough assessment of your health history, eating habits, and goals before building your personalised plan.
How does delivery and pick-up work for supplements?
At checkout you can choose delivery (R80 flat rate nationwide) or free pick-up at our Cape Town location. We'll contact you to confirm the details. For out-of-stock items, click "Request Item" and we'll order it in and contact you when it arrives.
How do I receive my meal plans and PDF guides?
Digital products are sent to your email address immediately after payment is confirmed. No shipping, no waiting.
Can I get a meal plan without a consultation?
Yes — our pre-made meal plans and guidelines are available directly from the Shop. For a fully personalised plan built around your specific health history, we recommend booking a consultation first.
