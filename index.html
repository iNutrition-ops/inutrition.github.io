FAQ

Common Questions

How do virtual consultations work?
After you book, we send you a secure video call link. No software to install — it works in your browser. Sessions are the same length and quality as in-person visits, and you receive your plan digitally afterward.
How does payment work for consultations?
Consultations are not paid online. After your session, we send you a detailed invoice which you submit to your medical aid for reimbursement. Most major South African medical aids cover registered dietitian consultations.
How long is an initial dietitian session?
Initial sessions are typically 60 minutes — enough time for a thorough assessment of your health history, eating habits, and goals before building your personalised plan.
How does delivery and pick-up work for supplements?
At checkout you can choose delivery (R80 flat rate nationwide) or free pick-up at our Cape Town location. We'll contact you to confirm the details. For out-of-stock items, click "Request Item" and we'll order it in and contact you when it arrives.
How do I receive my meal plans and PDF guides?
Digital products are sent to your email address immediately after payment is confirmed. No shipping, no waiting.
Can I get a meal plan without a consultation?
Yes — our pre-made meal plans and guidelines are available directly from the Shop. For a fully personalised plan built around your specific health history, we recommend booking a consultation first.
